Launch Date - YouTubers and influencers will be allocated 100 Piggies each to record marketing content. - Big YouTubers and influencers will giveaway a total of 5000 Piggies prior to launch for receivers to stake as early access. - Piggies can be used from (***Saturday, February 12, 2022 1:00:00 PM GMT onwards+++)

The value of Piggies will rise as more users invest BUSD.

Process
1.1. Enter BUSD into the piggy bank machine and earn daily staking rewards in Piggy Bank tokens (4% per day). You can claim your Piggy Bank rewards at anytime. You cannot unstake your BUSD.
1.2. You will earn Piggies as a percentage of BUSD invested irrespective of the Piggy Price.
1.3. i.e. If you invest 100 BUSD you will receive 4 Piggies per day. per day

2. You have two options with your Piggies, you can sell them for BUSD, or you can stake them to earn more Piggies (6% per day). Piggies staking is locked for 7 days.

3. Terminology
3.1. Total Supply : The maximum amount of Piggies that can exist
3.2. Circulating Supply : The amount of Piggies that are currently in wallets.
3.3. Available Supply : (Total Supply - Circulating Supply)
3.4. Piggy Price : (Total BUSD Balance / Available Supply) Mint Piggies - As you claim Piggies from minting, it is removed from the Available Supply and added to the Circulating Supply

4. Sell Piggies - As you sell Piggies, it is removed from the Circulating Supply and added to the Available Supply DYOR! As this is the first one of these that we are deploying, there is always a risk of the uncertain. Only use funds you can afford to lose.
